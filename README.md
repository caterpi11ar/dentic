# Dentic — Minimalist Brushing Guide

> Build a lifelong brushing habit the scientific way.

A **mobile-first web app** designed for phones — brush with one hand, follow the screen with your eyes.

[中文](./README.zh.md)

---

## Product Overview

Most people share the same three brushing problems: **missed zones, insufficient time, and poor technique**. Dentic is built to solve all three — targeting health-conscious users who want every brush session to actually count.

---

## Core Experience

### 1. Immersive 3D Oral Navigation

An interactive 3D tooth model powered by **Three.js** sits at the center of the screen, highlighting the current cleaning zone in real time. Just follow the screen — the app tells you exactly where to brush and how, no memorization needed.

### 2. Scientific Segmented Timer

The full brushing routine is broken into precise zones, each with its own countdown and automatic transition. A gentle audio cue signals each switch — no need to watch the screen.

### 3. Check-in & Stats

Log morning and evening sessions, track your streak on a monthly calendar, and get a quality score for every brush. The sense of progress makes consistency feel natural.

---

## Science Behind It

Dentic is built on the **Bass Method** — the gold standard in dental hygiene — translating its 7 steps into guided visual cues. See [docs/brushing-method.md](./docs/brushing-method.md)

**Data-driven habit formation** — The lightest possible check-in loop, making consistency easy and health measurable.
